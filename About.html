About Me


Learner - Former Operations Manager - Aspiring Designer - Chocoholic - Mom


Hello, Richelle here. As a career-changer, now a graphic design student, and a striving learner, I always try to challenge myself to be better at my craft, even if it could mean realizing that some parts of the experience, I may not be well suited for.
